To bolster the burgeoning trend of Online education, we introduce an attendance record management system which is equipped 
with functionalities such as real time face recognition, dynamic configuration
settings and many more.

The system is capable of working with platforms like Microsoft Teams and Zoom.


The system uses two methods of evaluation viz Weighted Averages and Top N
approach

Get ready to enjoy a full pleasure of attending the classes without the
disturbance or overhead of marking the attendance manually.
